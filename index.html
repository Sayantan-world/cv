Hi! I'm a third-year PhD student in the Computer Science and Engineering Department at The State University of New York at Buffalo, advised by Professor Rohini K. Srihari. My research focuses on conversational AI, specifically building personalized large language models (LLMs) that adapt to user-specific traits and interactions. I am passionate about advancing dialogue systems that feel natural, empathetic, and human-like.

These days, I am working on designing AI agents capable of intrinsically capturing personality traits and tailoring their responses accordingly. Recently, we published a paper at COLING 2025 titled "Beyond Discrete Personas: Personality Modeling Through Journal Intensive Conversations", which explores how continuous personality traits can be modeled in dialogue systems. Feel free to check out the project!

My interest in personalization began in the summer of 2023 during a collaborative study with experts in the Communication and Disability Department at the University at Buffalo, led by Dr. Jeffry Higginbotham. We aimed to create conversational systems for individuals with non-standard speech, helping them communicate more effectively and authentically. This project focused on empowering differently-abled individuals by developing AI systems that could speak on their behalf with greater nuance and expressiveness. A portion of this work was recently published at the EMNLP 2024 Workshop - CustomNLP4U.

Previously, I had the great fortune of working with accomplished research scientists and collaborating with industry experts during my undergraduate studies, which helped me build a strong foundation for my research career. I had the opportunity to work with brilliant mentors including Dr. Miaga Chang (MITACS Globalink Internship, Canada), Dr. Prosenjit Gupta (Elahe Technologies Summer Internship, India), Dr. Pabitra Mitra (IIT-KGP Summer Internship, India), and Karthik Ragubathy (Amazon Alexa Student Influencer Program, India).

Apart from spending my time doing research, I hold a keen interest in music and playing instruments (Mohan Veena, Synth, Guitar, and more.)!

I am currently looking for summer 2025 internships in areas related to the personalization of LLMs and conversational agents. If my research interests align with your team, please don't hesitate to reach out!

For more details, feel free to check out my CV or drop me an email!
